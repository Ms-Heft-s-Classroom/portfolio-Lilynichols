Music That I Like!

  • Evermore By Tayor Swift


    • Champang Problems
    Tolerate It
    No Body, No Crime
    Taylor Swift-Wikipedia-BY

    Evermore is a folk-sounding with a kinda of walking in the woods while its fall feel to it. With this album it is definitely a good one to listen to when it's raining or you are calmer because of the feel of it, or if you like me you will listen to it no matter what because I listen to the most not uplifting music all of the time. But this album has some masterpieces on it like Champagne Problems and No Body No Crime and Tolerate It.

  • Folklore By Taylor Swift


    • Betty
    This is Me trying Seven
    Exile
    The Last Great American Dynasty
    Taylor Swift-Wikipedia-BY

    Folklore is another folk-sounding album, it has a really nice cozy vibe to it and I think this is one of Taylor's best albums lyrically! I love this album sooo much, it has so many songs that me and any other Swifty loves like Betty, Seven, Exile, The Last Great American Dynasty and This is Me Trying

  • Wake Up And Its Over By Lovejoy


    • Warsaw
    Its Golden Hour Somewhere
    Lovejoy-Wikipedia-BY

    Wake Up and Its Over is an album by Lovejoy which is one of my favorite bands EVER. They make indie punk music with a bit of rock influence, If you listen to the Artic MOnkeys or Los cappesinos! they take influence from both of those bands. Some of my favorite songs from this album are It's Golden Hour Somewhere and Warsaw

  • Pebble Brain by Lovejoy


    • Perfume
    The Fall
    Concrete
    Lovejoy-Wikipedia-BY

    Pebble Brain is an album also by Lovejoy, I really love this album because of nostalgia and the overall vibe. This is an indie-punk kind of album with a little bit of pop influence also. It has some really good songs like Perfume,, The Fall and Concrete!

  • Did You Know THeres A Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd By Lana Del Rey


    • A&W Peppers
    Paris, Texas
    Candy Necklace
    Lana Del Rey-Wikipedia-BY

    Did You Know There's A Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd is an album by Lana del Rey, It is a 70's-inspired folk and soul-type music. Lana is a really talented lyricist and she really shows that in this album, she has so much emotion and so many things that are relatable. She talks about being in love and loving someone but also mentions political issues that I really really love her for talking about. Some of my favorite songs off of the album are A&W, Peppers, Paris Texas, and Candy Necklace.
